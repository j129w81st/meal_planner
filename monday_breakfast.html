Meal Details

Oatmeal with Berries

prep tips:

  • Prepare oats the night before for easier mornings.
  • Use frozen berries if fresh are unavailable.

get ahead:

  • Make a batch of oatmeal for multiple days.

quick substitutions:

  • Use almond milk instead of regular milk.
  • Swap berries for sliced bananas.

extra thoughts:

  • Oatmeal is a great source of fiber.
  • Adding nuts can boost protein content.

order of operations:

  • Heat oats with milk or water.
  • Top with berries and honey.
  • Serve warm.
