Meal Details

Grilled Chicken Salad

prep tips:

  • Grill extra chicken to use in future meals.
  • Prepare the dressing ahead of time.

get ahead:

  • Wash and chop lettuce in advance.

quick substitutions:

  • Use tofu instead of chicken for a vegetarian option.

extra thoughts:

  • This salad is packed with protein and vitamins.

order of operations:

  • Grill chicken and slice.
  • Assemble salad with greens and toppings.
  • Drizzle dressing and serve.
